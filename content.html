Life Below Water

The ocean covers more than 70% of our planet and supports countless marine species.It plays a vital role in regulating climate, providing food, and sustaining biodiversity. However, human activities threaten its delicate balance. Pollution, climate change, and Overfishing are endangering marine ecosystem. Protecting and restoring our ocean is crucial for future generations.
