Ocean Overview

The ocean covers more than 70% of our planet and is home to an incredible diversity of life forms‚Äîfrom microscopic plankton to the largest animal ever to have lived, the blue whale. Our understanding of ocean ecosystems continues to evolve as researchers explore the depths with advanced technologies and techniques.

  • Scientists estimate that over 80% of the ocean remains unmapped and unexplored
  • Researchers discover approximately 2,000 new marine species each year
  • The deepest part of the ocean, the Mariana Trench, reaches nearly 11,000 meters below sea level
  • Ocean research contributes to advances in medicine, renewable energy, and climate science
  • Marine technology is evolving rapidly, with autonomous vehicles now able to explore previously inaccessible areas
