Why Ocean Health Matters

Our oceans play a crucial role in regulating climate, providing food, and supporting diverse ecosystems. However, human activities have led to pollution, overfishing, and habitat destruction, threatening ocean health.

  • The ocean generates over 50% of the oxygen we breathe.
  • It absorbs about 30% of human-produced carbon dioxide.
  • More than 3 billion people depend on marine biodiversity for their livelihood.
  • Oceans regulate global temperature and weather patterns.
