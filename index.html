Our Mission

"To conserve and sustainably use the world’s oceans, seas, and marine resources by reducing pollution, protecting marine biodiversity, and promoting responsible fishing. This includes restoring ocean health, supporting coastal communities, and ensuring a sustainable future through education, innovation, and global cooperation."
